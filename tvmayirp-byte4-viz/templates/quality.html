Completeness

A large number of meteorites were marked with mass zero because that data is not known. Hence, these incomplete meterite records had to be ignored for my visualization. In spite of this gap, however, it was easy to see the increasing sparsity of exponentially heavier meteorites.

Similarly, 96.5% of the data is "Found" as opposed to "Fell". For the found meteorites, the year of impact/ age is uncertain. This indicates the that the "Year" field for a large chunk of the data is meaningless and incomplete.

Coherence

The above Network graphs cluster the meteorite data very well by material, with respect to mass of the meteorites and the location where the meteorite fell/ was found. This is coherent with our expectation.
